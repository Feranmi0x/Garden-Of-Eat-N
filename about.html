About Garden Of Eat'n

Compassion International is a non-profit that supports hundreds of children in third world countries. They advocate pairing compassionate people with children living in extreme poverty, in order to release the children from emotional, financial, social and resource poverty. Garden of Eat'n continues to support the Compassion community through a sponsorship that allows opportunities for learning and growing physically, mentally and spiritually.

What's the little story about Garden Of Eat'n?

We source and pick our ingredients from local farms, so you can enjoy the freshest and most flavorful meals possible. We believe that good food starts with good ingredients, which is why we're committed to using only the best and healthiest ingredients in our cooking. Our mission is to leave our community a little healthier than we found it. We offer quality food and service from Creekside Town Center, Roseville, Blue Oaks Plaza, Roseville, and Auburn Plaza, Auburn Locations.
